How to Generate an App-Specific Password

  1. Go to appleid.apple.com and sign in
  2. Navigate to Security section
  3. Under App-Specific Passwords, click Generate
  4. Enter a label like "VisMatrix Calendar Sync"
  5. Copy the generated password (format: xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx)
  6. Paste it in the field above

Security: Your credentials are stored securely and never shared.

Note: Standard iCloud passwords don't work. You must use an app-specific password.
