Privacy & Security

Your data is safe with us. We're transparent about how we use your information.

What Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, email, and password (encrypted)
  • Activity Data: Tasks, logs, plans, and categories you create
  • Google Calendar Data: Calendar events (only if you choose to connect)
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the app to improve your experience

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide the Service: Store and display your tasks, logs, and analytics
  • Google Calendar Integration: Sync events to create activity logs (read-only access)
  • Social Features: Enable sharing with friends you connect with
  • Improve Experience: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality

Google Calendar Integration

Purpose: VisMatrix requests access to your Google Calendar to automatically sync calendar events as activity logs, helping you track time spent on scheduled activities.

✓ What We DO:

  • • Read your calendar events
  • • Sync events to your logs
  • • Store encrypted OAuth tokens
  • • Allow you to disconnect anytime

✗ What We DON'T DO:

  • • Modify or delete your events
  • • Share your calendar data
  • • Create new calendar events
  • • Access other Google services
VisMatrix's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.
